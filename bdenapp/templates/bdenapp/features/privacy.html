POLICY AGREEMENT FOR PROPERTY OWNERS

  1. Landlords must provide accurate property details, clearly state house rules, pricing, cancellation policies and lease terms of their property.
  2. Every property listed should be rightly stated either its for sale, lease or peer-to-peer room sharing so to enable renters or buyers be accurate on their choice either during purchasing or renting.
  3. To every transaction carried out, a notification or review should be sent to B-den platform to enable growth on the platform and monitoring of the property either to removal on visibility on the platform
  4. Handover over of property right should be notified to the B-den admin for change of property owner profile on B-den database.
  5. An agreement policy on management of property on B-den platform requires a particular percentage pending on the duration of time the property is hosted
  6. Property must agree to lease term before committing.
  7. Every property listed on B-den site must be registered as it follows Nigeria law, any form of stolen property discovered by B-den will be reported to the Authorities and ceased. So therefore, all property listed should be genuine.
  8. Property owners must agree to the terms and agreement of B-den before having the right to list their properties
  9. No agency fee, security or any other consolidated fee in B-den platform, so therefore any amount set for any property covers every payment. i.e. (one-in-all payment)
  10. Phone numbers and email provided should be 24/7 available for property renters and buyers should be able to reach out.
  11. Properties must be clean, safe and fully functional for renters

POLICY AGREEMENT FOR RENTERS

  1. Tenants must review and agree to lease terms before committing.
  2. Rent must be paid on time to landlord to avoid sold out and if its peer-to-peer rent part payment is made pending interval of three month and the other peer hasn’t paid then it will be refunded.
  3. Tenants must maintain the property and report any damages immediately.
  4. For Peer-to-Peer room sharing be sure of the PROFILE of who you agree to stay with for B-den or property owner won’t be held responsible for any wrong choice of roommate.
  5. Make sure the property owner responds before payment is carried out.
  6. Any damages on the property before packing must be reported immediately so to be fixed by the property owner

CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

B-DEN applies a standardized cancellation policy for short-term rentals unless otherwise specified by the property owner Flexible: Full refund if cancelled at least 48 hours before check-in. Moderate: 50% refund if cancelled 7 days before check-in. Strict: No refund for cancellations made within 14 days of check-in. Property owners must select one of these policies to ensure consistency across the platform. Any property already hosted and deleted by the property owner attracts a fine

POLICY AGREEMENT FOR SELLERS

Sellers must provide accurate property details and legal ownership documents. Listings must specify whether prices are negotiable or fixed. Any commission or service fees owed to B-DEN for successful sales must be paid as agreed

POLICY AGREEMENT FOR BUYERS

Buyers are responsible for verifying all property details before making payments. Buyers should conduct due diligence including property inspection and document verification. B-DEN may facilitate escrow services for secure transactions, but buyers and sellers must agree to escrow terms before proceeding

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

B-DEN provides a dispute resolution system for issues between landlords, tenants, buyers, and sellers. If no resolution is reached through B-DEN’s mediation, parties may pursue legal action under Nigerian property laws

LIABILITY & PLATFORM DISCLAIMER

B-DEN is a facilitator, not a direct party in rental or sales agreements. B-DEN does not own it only manages the properties listed on the platform. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with local property laws and agreements.

TERMINATION & VIOLATIONS

B-DEN reserves the right to suspend or remove users who violate these terms. Fraudulent listings, non-payment or repeated complaints can result in account termination.
